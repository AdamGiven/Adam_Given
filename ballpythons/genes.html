Genes

The three traits are Homozygous, Heterozygous, and Normal. A normal Ball Python is neither showing or carrying the recessive gene. A Heterozygous Ball Python is carrying the recessive trait but does not show the trait. A Homozygous(recessive) Ball Python Shows and Carries the recessive trait. An example of a recessive trait would be an Albino Ball Python. Below is a chart to show the likely offspring.
